NDS Stormwater Solutions

NDSStormwater Solutions

Valley Construction Supply is pleased to introduce a New Product line. NDS is an innovative brand whose products specialize in Stormwater Solutions and Onsite Stormwater Management. We wanted to share some information with you about these products and how they will benefit you.

NDS StormChambers provides you another option for meeting your underground stormwater requirements on both commercial and residential projects. They are easy to install, can be maintained and are very economical compared to some other underground stormwater solutions.

Additional Drainage Solutions:

Features & Benefits of StormChambers

The NDS StormChamaber is the most cost-effective chamber solution on the market.

  1. Cost Savings: Reduced man hours - Less equipment needed for installation
  2. Product Availability: We have product in stock to support projects immediately
  3. Stackable StormChambers: Reduces required footprint allowing for more site flexibility
  4. DesignWorx Team: Layout Designs and Engineering support in 1 - 2 days versus weeks

What To Expect From NDS StormChambers

    Installation

  • Built in end walls
  • No header pipe manifold system required
  • Flexible design
  • Lowest installed cost

    Maintenance

  • Differentiated and centralized sediment control through our propriety SedimenTrap™
  • SedimenTrap™ gathers sediments and other pollutants in one place for quick, easy and inexpensive removal by vacuum truck

    Longevity

  • Withstands the most extreme environments
  • Material bends instead of breaks giving it a brittleness temperature of -107°F vs. industry average of -2°F
  • 100 Year Life Expectancy

    Multiple Layers

  • 34" Chambers: Capable of 3-layer stacking
  • 44" Chambers: Contact NDS DesignWorx for any projects that may need stacking
  • We offer expert site-specific design guidance based on 20 years of successful completion of projects
