Concrete Accessories

Wholesale Concrete Accessories

Every concrete “project” has both simple as well as challenging assembly requirements. Varying wall heights and thicknesses, slab construction (both with or without) insulation/vapor barrier requirements, grade beams, thickened footings, varying “pier” sizes and depth’s all require an array of supplies to allow you to complete your project. Valley Construction Supply has in stock (or quick access to) a wide variety of concrete construction accessories. Form cleats, snap-ties (fixed flat washer, plastic cone etc.), rigid insulation (various “R” value and thicknesses available), under slab vapor barriers, tie wire, duplex nails are just the tip of the iceberg of supplies Valley Construction Supply can provide for your project.
