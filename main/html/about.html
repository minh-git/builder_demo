About Valley Construction Supply

Valley's History

In 1982 Cliff Beatty founded Valley Construction Supply with the sole purpose of providing the region’s contractors with a reliable source for building materials at competitive prices. Being built on the ideals of honesty, dedication and leadership, almost 40 years later Valley Construction Supply is still family-owned and operated by Cliff’s son, Mark Beatty, who continues with the same values.

With a fleet of 25 trucks and two conveniently located warehouses, Pacific and Marysville, we are able to provide our customers with next-day delivery. Our dedicated sales staff has an average of 25 years experience which gives them the ability to react to our customer’s needs. Valley Construction Supply is committed to putting customer service as a top priority in an ever-changing building environment.

Need a contractor? We know a few.

Our vetted network is the place to start. If you are in need of a quality foundation, excavation or framing contractor, our sales staff can refer high-quality contractors in the greater Puget Sound region. We do the same for our loyal customers seeking the best professionals for their projects.
