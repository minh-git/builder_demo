Rebar Fabrication

Valley's Rebar Fabrication Facility

Valley Construction Supply can provide rebar fabrication for your residential and commercial jobs. Using the ASA standard rebar bend list, we can cut and bend rebar to fill your custom orders. Located in our Pacific store, we can deliver your fabricated rebar orders right to your jobsite. From detailing, estimating and fabricating, our experienced sales staff is here to help every step of the way.
