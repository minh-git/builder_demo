Outside Sales Position

Compensation: competitive base salary + commission / DOE, and beneifts

Want to be part of a team where we value hard work and dedication and have a culture that rewards you with growth, development and internal promotions? Do you work well with others and are able to work in a fast-paced environment? Do you possess great organization skills, interpersonal skills, are a self-starter and communicate well both verbally and in writing?

POSITION OVERVIEW

Valley construction supply in Maryville is seeking an experienced, self-motivated outside sales person focusing on acquisition of new customer accounts, as well as helping maintain the relationships we have with existing customers.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Understand customer needs and requirements.
  • Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate sales executives for further development and closure.
  • Maintain and expand your database of prospects within your assigned territory.
  • Working closely with the Inside Sales Staff to provide support by relieving them of administrative work. Duties may include order processing/entry, making price quotations, record keeping, RFP response, product training, or other related tasks.
  • Working closely with the purchasing manager to ensure all info is correct before purchasing/creating the sales order’s.

REQUIREMENTS

  • High School Diploma or General Education Degree (GED)
  • Have a valid driver's license.
  • Able to pass a drug screening
  • Ability to maintain and curate relationships with a network of customers, contractors, and suppliers
  • Strong communication, and time management skills.
  • Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage time effectively
  • 2 years’ experience in construction preferred
  • Strong listening and presentation skills.
  • Computer skills. (Familiar with Word/Excel)

BENEFITS

We offer a competitive benefit package including a 401K plan, medical, dental, life and disability insurance. We are an Equal Opportunity Employer. Candidates are subject reference checks, criminal background and DMV checks, assessments, and a pre-employment drug screening. EOE/M/F/Vets/Disability.
